I’m a 23-year-old, half-Filipina, half-Guyanese creative from Montreal, Canada. Growing up, I was always close to my brothers, which naturally led me to share their interests—especially in video games, anime, and all things nerd culture. These early influences shaped my passion for storytelling, design, and interactive media, inspiring me to explore the intersection of art and technology.

As I’ve grown, these interests fueled my newfound passion for game programming and design, leading me to explore tools like Godot and Unity, while also experimenting with languages like JavaScript, HTML, CSS and React Native.

Moving forward, I aim to push the boundaries of interactive media, using my skills to create immersive experiences that combine storytelling and technology, with the aim of entering the video game industry as a game programmer/designer or a software engineer.
